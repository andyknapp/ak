Before moving into web development, I spent more than a decade in advertising - specifically media planning - across Maxus, MindShare, Mediaedge, and Brouillard Communications (all current or former WPP companies). My last role was Partner, Group Planning Director at Maxus in NYC, where I led the team on Church &amp; Dwight consumer brands. I worked in traditional media but spent a lot of time in the then-emerging digital space, including social strategy and custom program development. That background taught me client and team management, public speaking, and an analytical bent - all of which I lean on regularly in my engineering work today.
